The UTEC

The University of Toronto Electronic Computer Mark I

The UTEC's legacy as the first electronic computer in Canada has almost been lost to time. Its title is often given to the Ferranti Mark I, a computer purchased from Britain by the Univerity of Toronto which eventually led to them abandon the UTEC before it could ever be fully developed.

The UTEC and the University of Toronto's contributions to the Electronic Computer Revolution are fascinating parts of Canadian history which warrent further study and celebration.
